The Manifesto

Why we exist and how to use the grill.

Philosophy

RoastMe isn't just an app; it's a digital reality check. In a world of "nice" AI assistants, we decided to build something with a backbone. We use Gemini 2.0 Flash to analyze your mistakes and deliver them with elite levels of sarcasm.

The Workflow

1. Input: Paste your code or text on the home screen.
2. Settings: Choose your Persona and Savagery level in the settings drawer.
3. Execution: Hit 'Ignite the Grill' and watch the truth unfold.

Exporting

Every roast generates a Unique Case ID. You can export your verdict as a luxury-grade PNG receipt to share on social media or send to your therapist.
